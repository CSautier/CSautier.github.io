I am a Ph.D. student in Imagine, the research vision group at Ecole des Ponts Paristech under the supervision of Vincent Lepetit, and work jointly at valeo.ai with Gilles Puy, Alexandre Boulch, and Renaud Marlet. My research focuses on self-supervised and unsupervised learning for Lidar point clouds, with a specific interest in multimodality and principal applications for autonomous driving.
